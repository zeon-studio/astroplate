Write your post in Markdown.

Start with a short introduction that tells the reader what they will learn and why it matters.

## First main section

Use clear H2 sections for search intent and H3 sections for supporting points. The article page builds its table of contents automatically from H2 and H3 headings.

### Supporting point

Keep paragraphs concise, answer the reader's next question, and add internal links to related posts when relevant.

## SEO checklist

- Use `draft: true` while writing.
- Rename the file to control the final URL.
- Keep images in `public/images/` and reference them with absolute paths like `/images/your-cover-image.png`.
- Replace placeholder descriptions with a specific one-sentence summary.
- Use categories as topic hubs and tags only for useful cross-links.
